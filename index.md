## About Me

Rui Shi is an AI Engineer at Huawei CBG, where he focuses on cutting-edge research in multimodal visual generation and post-training techniques. He received his B.S. degree in 2022 and his M.S. degree in 2025, both from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), under the fortunate supervision of [Prof. Bingbing Ni](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=eUbmKwYAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao) and Prof. Xiangzhong Fang. Prior to joining Huawei, he gained valuable industry experience through research internships at Tencent AI Lab and Taobao and Tmall Group (Alibaba).

Outside of his research and engineering work, Rui is an avid traveler and an enthusiastic photographer, always keen on capturing the beauty of the world through his lens.


## Research Interests

- **Multimodal Visual Generation & Editing**
- **Post-training for Visual Generative Models**
- **3D Vision**
